Climate warming from human activity is already impacting many natural systems and changing our enviroment.

By visuallising invisible climate changes as interactive explorations, we hope to raise awareness for the little things that will have a huge impact on our future.

Make a Change

Here are a few simple things that you can do to make a impact.

Live car free

Save 2.4 tons of CO2 per year

Try selling your car and taking public transport, riding a bicycle or walking as your primary means of transportation.

Find out More ↗

Avoid one
transatlantic flight

Save 1.6 tons of CO2 per year

Try short distance travel by train or bus on your next holiday. Long distance fligths are high-impact CO2 polluters.

Find out More ↗

Buy green energy

Save 1.5 tons of CO2 per year

Try changing your energy provider from coal or natural gas to a renewable wind or solar provider.

Find out More ↗

Plant-based diet

Save 0.8 tons of CO2 per year

Try reducing or replacing animal products in your diet with plant based ingredients that require sell resources to grow & process.

Find out More ↗

Wash clothes in cold water

Save 0.3 tons of CO2 per year

Try using the cold cycle on your next load of washing. This uses less energy and will make your clothes last longer.
